## Healthcare Risk Register (Excel)

A healthcare-focused risk register template covering clinical, operational, information security, privacy, and vendor/third-party risks. Includes risk scoring, controls/mitigations, owners, and status tracking.

**Download:**

-  [Direct download](https://github.com/AwineAkanlerige/healthcare-risk-register-template/blob/main/docs/Healthcare_Risk_Register_Awine_final.xlsx)

**How to use:**
- Add risks as they are identified and keep owners + due dates current.
- Review and update likelihood/impact regularly (e.g., monthly/quarterly).
- Track mitigation actions and residual risk after controls.
